A classic delicious American hot dog

The classic American beef hot dog, wrapped in a perfectly baked bun cooked to just the right temperature

Many Americans prefer hot dogs burnt but many also prefer them to be cooked just right and topped with the classic combination of ketchup and mustard. Delicious!

Hot Dog Recipe!

Steps

  1. Begin to cook a hot dog from your local grocery store, (boil or grill)
  2. At the same time toast your hot dog buns, face down
  3. Now toast the buns face up, make sure to butter the pan or grill
  4. Finally set your cooked hot dog in your crispy bun
  5. Alas, add your preferred sauces I prefer ketchup and mustard!
  6. Enjoy!
